Towards the end of my time as a PhD student in Bath I helped to deliver workshops on the role of data and databases in materials discovery.

This was part of the EPSRC CDT in New and Sustainable Photovoltaics so was centred around materials properties such as band gap and effective mass.

I put together several Jupyter notebooks for learning how to interact with various materials databases.
