Lab demonstrating during my time as a PhD student involved helping first and second year undergraduates get to grips with compchem fundamental such as geometry relaxation and forcefields.

There was also a FORTRAN programming course for final year chemists led by Prof Steve Parker. This was a challenging but highly rewarding course to be involved in, because it was most of the students' first exposure to programming.
